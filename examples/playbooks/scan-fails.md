# Playbook: Scan Fails to Start

When a scan will not start, work through these steps in order.

## 1. Check device connection

Confirm the device shows as connected in the status bar. If not, reseat the USB
cable and power-cycle the device.

## 2. Check calibration

A missing or expired calibration profile blocks scanning. Re-run the calibration
wizard if the diagnostics panel reports "uncalibrated".

## 3. Check disk space

Scans need free disk space. Free at least 2GB and retry.
